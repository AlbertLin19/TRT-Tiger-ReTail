Frequently Asked Questions

Note: If you have questions that are not addressed on this page, please submit them via our "Contact Us" page!

I clicked “purchase” on an item. What now?

The item will appear on your “My Purchases” page with the message “awaiting seller confirmation”. Once the seller of your item confirms the purchase, reach out to the seller with the provided email address to plan how the two of you can exchange the item and money!

I need to edit information about an item I posted earlier. How do I do that?

Navigate to the “My Items” page. As long as your item is not currently involved in a transaction, there will be an option to edit information about any of your items.

Why is there no option to purchase some of the items on the gallery page?

A frozen item on the gallery page is currently in the process of being purchased by another buyer. If this transaction doesn’t go through, the item will be reinstated on the image gallery as a purchasable item. Check back in a couple of days to see if the item is up for sale!
